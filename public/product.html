Common Questions

How do I wash the Somni Contour?
Detach the electronic module (3 seconds), then machine wash the fabric shell in cold water. Air dry. The memory foam core is rated for 50+ wash cycles.
Does it work for side sleepers?
Yes — the 0.8mm ultra-thin speakers and 3D contoured shape are specifically designed to be comfortable in all sleep positions, including side sleeping.
What's the Bluetooth range?
Bluetooth 6.0 provides up to 10 meters (33 feet) of stable connection. Pair once and it auto-connects each night.
Can I wear it with lash extensions?
Absolutely. The 15mm-deep eye cups are specifically designed to provide full clearance for eyelash extensions, long natural lashes, and blinking.
